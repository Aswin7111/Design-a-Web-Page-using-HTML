Lionel Messi

Lionel Messi is an Argentine professional footballer widely considered one of the greatest players of all time. Born in Rosario, Argentina, on June 24, 1987.

Playing Style and Achievements

Messi is known for his dribbling, close control, agility, and goal scoring ability. He is predominantly left-footed and delivers precise passes and goals.

Key Achievements

  • Eight Ballon d'Or awards
  • Most goals in La Liga history (474)
  • Most assists in La Liga history (192)
  • Most official assists in football history (385)
  • First to win eight European Golden Shoes
  • Over 860 career goals
    • Barcelona: 672 goals
    • Argentina: 108 goals
    • PSG & Inter Miami: remaining goals

Trophies

  1. FC Barcelona: 10 La Liga, 7 Copa del Rey, 8 Super Cups, 4 UCL, 3 Super Cups (UEFA), 3 Club World Cups
  2. PSG: 2 Ligue 1 titles, 1 Trophée des Champions
  3. Inter Miami: 1 MLS Leagues Cup, 1 Supporters' Shield
  4. Argentina: 2 Copa América, 1 FIFA World Cup, Olympic Gold
